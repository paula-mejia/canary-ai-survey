→ The initiative

Mapping

In the spirit of contributing to this collective learning, we're launching the Canary AI Adoption Survey, an open initiative for VC-backed startups across the ecosystem.

The goal is to map how teams are adapting their work, their processes, and the tools they are using to navigate this new reality and bring more efficiency to their operations.

Because AI adoption looks different depending on the function leading it, we've designed four tailored versions of the survey. We'd love to count on your participation by filling out the form designed for your role, and on your help in spreading the word to peers and leaders adapting to AI within their own functions.

Pick your role

Four surveys.
One per function.

AI adoption looks different depending on the function leading it. Each version takes about 7 minutes and is designed around the questions that matter most for your day-to-day. Choose the one that fits.
